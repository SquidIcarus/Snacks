How to make spiced popcorn

  1. clarify butter
  2. combine spice mixture
      3. salt
      pepper
      cayenne
      garlic granules
      onion powder
  3. pop popcorn
  4. drizzle and toss with clarified butter
  5. sprinkle in spice mixture as you toss
  6. add more or less to taste
  7. eat with chopsticks to avoid spicy fingers
  8. enjoy!
